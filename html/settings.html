Settings Manager

Greeting Message Send automatic messages at the start of each chat to greet each new stranger!
Typing Speed Choose the speed in words-per-minute (WPM) to type your greeting message.
Starting Delay Choose how much time to wait before starting to type your greeting in chat.
Sending Delay Choose how much time to wait after typing your greeting in chat before sending it.
Auto-Skip Skip to the next chat after a configurable number of seconds has passed.
Auto-Reconnect (Text Only) Automatically enter the next chat after your current one ends when in text mode.
IP Grabbing (Video Only) Display the IP address of the person you are chatting with when in video mode.
IP Geolocation (Video Only) Display the location of the person you are chatting with when in video mode.
Skip Repeats (Video Only) Skip people who you have chatted with before on Omegle when in video mode.
Blocked IPs (Video Filter) Skip to the next chat after a configurable number of seconds has passed.
Sexual (Video Filter) (Beta Version) Detect NSFW video from your partner and automatically block their video input.
Sexual (Text Filter) Filter messages of a sexual nature sent by you or your partner in the text chat.
Profanity (Text Filter) Filter messages with profane words sent by you or your partner in the text chat.
Themes Pick between one of the three themes to customize your Omegle experience.
Header Buttons Enable or disable the language & share buttons at the top-right of the page.
Screenshot Button Toggle the screenshot button over your partner's video when in video mode.
Message Toolbar Toggle the message toolbar for a list of custom values to paste in your chatbox.
