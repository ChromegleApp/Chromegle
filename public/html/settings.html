Settings Manager

Greeting Send an automatic message at the start of each chat to greet each stranger!
Greeting Delays Choose how much time to wait before starting/sending your greeting.
Paste Hotbar Toggle the paste hotbar for a list of custom values to paste in your chatbox.
Sexual Filter Filter messages of a sexual nature sent by you or your partner in the text chat.
Profanity Filter Filter messages with profane words sent by you or your partner in the text chat.
Blocked IP List List the IP addresses you have blocked when in the video chat.
Blocked Countries Skip to the chat if a user belongs to a given country code when in video chat
Skip Repeats Skip people who you have chatted with before on Omegle when in video chat.
Fullscreen Tool Get a fullscreen overlay button when in the video chat.
Screenshot Tool Get a screenshot overlay button when in the video chat.
Video-Popout Tool Get a popout overlay button when in the video chat.
Auto-Reconnect Automatically start the next chat after your current one ends when in text mode.
Auto-Skip Words Skip when someone sends a specific word in the first 5 messages in text chat.
Auto-Skip Age Skip when someone sends an age outside the configured range in text chat.
Auto-Skip Timer Skip to the next chat after a configurable number of seconds has passed.
Voice Commands Do simple actions like send messages, skip and start chats with your voice!
Theme Modes Pick between one of the three themes to customize your Omegle experience.
Splash Image Have your very own custom splash image on the Omegle site.
Header Buttons Enable or disable the language & share buttons at the top-right of the page.
